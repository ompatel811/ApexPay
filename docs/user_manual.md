# User Manual - ApexPay Client Guide

Welcome to ApexPay! This guide walks you through using the customer application interface.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating your account
1.  Open the application at `https://localhost` (or the production URL provided by your administrator).
2.  Click **Get Started** or **Register**.
3.  Fill in a unique username, your email address, and a secure password.
4.  Once registered, sign in to land on the main dashboard page.

---

## 2. Interactive Dashboard Hub

The main dashboard provides a single overview of your financial status:
*   **Wallet Balance**: View your current liquid balance.
*   **Recent Transactions**: Review your payment logs, categorized automatically by category (e.g. food, utility, travel).
*   **Quick Actions**: Send Money, Scan QR, View Statements, or Request Refund.

---

## 3. Transferring Funds

### Direct Wallet Transfer (P2P)
1.  On the dashboard, click **Send Money**.
2.  Enter the recipient's **Account Number** (e.g., `AP8738271839`).
3.  Enter the amount and add a descriptive remark.
4.  Confirm the transaction. The funds will be debited instantly.

### Scanning QR Codes
1.  Click **Scan QR** to launch your device camera.
2.  Point the camera at an ApexPay merchant QR code.
3.  Confirm the parsed payment amount and click **Pay**.

---

## 4. AI Financial Insights & Chat

Use the **AI Assistant** tab to optimize your spending:
*   **Chat Box**: Ask the assistant questions like: *"How much did I spend on food this week?"* or *"Analyze my budget"*.
*   **Financial Health Score**: View your current calculated score (scale 0-100) and tips on how to improve it.
*   **Budget Recommendations**: The system generates personalized recommendations if you exceed specific categories.
